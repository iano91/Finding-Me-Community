Who We Are

Finding Me Community is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting mental health wellness across Africa, with a special focus on young adults. We create safe spaces where individuals can engage in authentic, vulnerable, and open conversations about their mental health, drawing on the Ubuntu philosophy of “I am because we are.” Our approach centers on the power of community-driven support systems to foster self-awareness, reduce stigma, and build emotional resilience.

Check out our core values

Mission

To create a community where people can engage from a place of authenticity, vulnerability, and openness as a pathway toward self-awareness for better mental health.

Vision

To be the leading organization in Africa advocating for mental health wellness through a community-based approach.

Our Core Principles

  • Peer-to-Peer Support: We believe that individuals thrive when they can lean on one another for support, encouragement, and understanding.
  • Ubuntu Philosophy: Inspired by the African philosophy, “I am because we are,” we emphasize the importance of interconnectedness and collective healing.
  • Sustainability through Impact: Our motto reflects our commitment to creating meaningful and lasting change by empowering individuals and communities to build resilient mental health practices.
  • Authenticity and Vulnerability: We encourage participants to show up as their true selves and engage in open conversations, knowing that this honesty is the key to healing and self-awareness.
  • Accessibility and Inclusivity: We are dedicated to making mental health resources available to underserved communities, particularly in Kenya and across Africa.

What We Do

Wellness Program

Our flagship Eight-Week Wellness Program brings young adults together in group therapy sessions led by trained peer counselors. Participants engage in a safe space to build self-awareness, resilience, and emotional intelligence.

Alumni Program

Graduates of our wellness program continue to receive support through peer-led groups, ongoing resources, and mentorship opportunities, fostering a lifelong commitment to mental wellness.

High School Mental Health Campaigns

We visit schools across Kenya to educate students and educators about managing stress, emotional well-being, and creating a supportive environment for mental health awareness.

Corporate Training Programs

We empower organizations with tools to create healthier work environments by promoting mental health and emotional intelligence.
