Overview of Finding Me Community

At Finding Me Community, we believe in the power of fostering mental well-being and purpose through community-driven support systems. Our corporate training programs are designed to help organizations cultivate environments that prioritize mental health, build resilience, and enhance team collaboration. By focusing on well-being, self-awareness, and peer support, we create pathways for employees to thrive both personally and professionally.

Our Corporate Training Programs

Mental Health & Well-being in the Workplace

Objective: Equip employees with practical tools to manage stress, improve mental resilience, and create a supportive work culture.

Topics Covered:

  • Understanding mental health and breaking down stigma
  • Techniques for stress management and mindfulness
  • Building resilience and emotional intelligence in the workplace
  • Creating a culture of care and support

Duration: 1-day workshop (6 hours)

Peer Support Systems for Enhanced Collaboration

Objective: Train employees to build internal support networks that foster collaboration and a stronger sense of belonging.

Topics Covered:

  • The power of peer-to-peer support in the workplace
  • Building trust and empathy among teams
  • Facilitating open conversations about challenges
  • Tools for setting up peer support groups within the organization

Duration: 2-day workshop (12 hours)
